Admission Information

Join Konika Cadet Academy and begin your journey towards success in cadet college entrance exams.

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Students from Class 5 to Class 8
  • Minimum academic performance in previous exams
  • Good physical fitness

Application Process

  1. Collect the admission form from our office or download it online.
  2. Fill in the required details with necessary documents.
  3. Submit the form before the deadline.

Important Dates

  • Form Distribution: 1st March 2025
  • Submission Deadline: 31st March 2025
  • Entrance Exam: 15th April 2025
